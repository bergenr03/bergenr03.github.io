Celeste

Description

Since its release in 2018, Celeste has become one of the most active and popular speedruns. Celeste is known for being an extremely difficult game and you die a lot, but it is very forgiving. The game is $20 and the first playthrough will take a few hours. The game encourages you as you play to not give up, and beating the game is very satisfying. This category has 6 main categories to choose from.

  • Any%: Beat the summit as fast as possible.
  • All Red Berries: Collect all of the strawberries in the game
  • True Ending: Beat the new DLC chapter known as "Farewell" as fast as possible.
  • All Hearts: Collect all of the Crystal Hearts in the game
  • All Chapters: Beat all of the Chapters in the game
  • 100%: Complete Everything in the game and collect everything in the game
