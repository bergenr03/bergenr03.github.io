My Top 3 Favorite Speedrunning Games

Super Mario 64

This game is an old one, but a fun one! For a game that game out in 1996, I think that it is very good for it's time. The game revolutionized the 3D aspect of video games and the movement is very good. I still think it has some of the best movement out of any video game.

Refunct

Refunct is a game that you can get for 3 dollars on steam and is also very short. Although it has a very short length, it is a very relaxing and calming game, and has great music! It even has a built in timer to see how fast you can beat it.

Celeste

Celeste is probably my favorite video game of all time. It tells a great story of a girl named Madelyn's inner self trying to stop her from climbing a mountain, but she perseveres. This game also has great music and is very difficult, but once you beat Celeste, you feel a great sense of accomplishment.
